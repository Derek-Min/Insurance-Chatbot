Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Shield AI

How quickly can I deploy RetailBot AI?

Most retail businesses can deploy Shield AI within 24-48 hours. Our streamlined onboarding process includes data integration, custom training on your product catalog, and comprehensive testing before going live.

Can RetailBot handle complex customer inquiries?

Absolutely. Our AI is trained on retail-specific scenarios including product recommendations, order management, returns processing, warranty inquiries, and technical support. For complex cases requiring human judgment, the system seamlessly escalates to your team.

What's the difference between Assistant and Partner access?

Assistant access provides customer support tools, basic analytics, and chat management. Partner access includes advanced business intelligence, system configuration, user management, revenue tracking, and strategic insights for decision-making.

How secure is my customer data?

Security is our top priority. We use AES-256 encryption, maintain SOC 2 Type II certification, comply with GDPR and CCPA, and undergo regular security audits. Customer data is never used for training other models or shared with third parties.

Can I customize the AI's responses and behavior?

Yes, extensively. You can customize the AI's tone, upload your brand guidelines, train it on your specific products and policies, set escalation rules, and create custom workflows. The system learns and adapts to your brand voice over time.
